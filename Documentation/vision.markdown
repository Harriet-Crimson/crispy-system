**Background**

Students are expected to stay on top of multiple deadlines from across their different modules, however they often struggle to effectively manage their time. This often stems from a lack of motivation, as they do not know how far they have progressed with their tasks. By not being able to follow their course, students’ grades can end up suffering.

**Project Goals**

The goal for this project is to make it easier for students to keep track of their coursework by helping them schedule tasks that they need to complete. The project will display planned tasks in an easily understandable  Gantt chart format.

**Primary Users and Stakeholders**

The main users of the software will be students at the UEA. While the application will only initially be offered to computing science students, it will eventually be offered to a wider range of students, so it should be usual for those with limited technical experience.

Other stakeholders include teaching staff, who want to track the progress of their students and members of the UEA IT department who will be responsible for  maintaining it.

**Success Criteria**

The project will be successful if students can add and edit activities with ease and also record their progress in different activities. A Gantt chart should be generated from student information, it should take  dependencies into account. Students should be more able to tell which tasks have higher priority, so that they can effectively manage their time around it.
