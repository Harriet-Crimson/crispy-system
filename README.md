 OVERVIEW  
UEA is always looking for ways to support students in their studies and with many of the 
modules and assignments requiring detailed planning, prioritisation and tracking of tasks, the 
university wants to you to develop the “Study Planner” application. The goal of the Study 
Planner is to help students schedule tasks based on their module schedule and to define tasks 
and their dependencies for which they can then track their progress. Initially this application 
will be offered by the School of Computing Sciences but support for other schools in the future 
should be considered.
